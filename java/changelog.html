A 2-in-1 update! 2 major features added

Note that this update is waiting for Tourney Group Game 1 to terminate to be released

Additions

  • Potions: Purchase them for temporary buffs!
  • AOE Spells: Cast a spell onto everyone on a single island!

Potions

  • During your turn, you May purchase and consume potions whenever you want!
  • Potions give you temporary buffs for some amount of buffs. However, most potions have a level requirement for them to be used

    • Note: This does NOT affect the "extra crit damage" perk from Strength - that stays the same (+1% Crit Damage per Strength)
  • Weather has been balanced
