2.8.1 Release

Posted 8:22pm on October 26, 2024


Why is this update like 10x larger than 2.8


Additions

  • Elemental Dust
    • Instead of upgrading, you get these when levelling up!
    • Can be used to increase damage of the corresponding element on a weapon/spell
    • You can combine two of the same tier together to create the next tier!
    • Higher tiers give more elemental damage when applied
    • There are 4 tiers, as follows:
      • Tier I: +1 Damage
      • Tier II: +3 Damage
      • Tier III: +7 Damage
      • Tier IV: +15 Damage

Changes

  • Modified level-ups to return them to pre-2.7.10 xp requirements
  • Multiple elements on a weapon no longer decreases the DPS
  • Weapons and spells can have up to 5 elements
  • New and updated splash text
  • Nerfed AP reward when levelling up
  • Decreased bridge building/burning costs
    • Building a free bridge now costs 4 instead of 6
    • Building a toll bridge now costs 8 instead of 12
    • Burning a bridge now costs 6 instead of 9
  • Decreased island building costs - Fixed cost of 5
  • You can no longer partially fund island building
  • Crossing a bridge decreases your stamina by a fixed value of 1
  • More lockout challenges - 2 of each rarity
  • Reroll amplifiers give a fixed rate of 5% boost to your roll

Removals

  • The ability to upgrade spells and weapons
  • "Defence" events

Fixes

  • BG-83: Game resets do not clear the event log nor reset the lockout challenges
  • BG-84: Plenty of floating point errors
  • BG-85: Lockout challenges reset before healing gets applied, leaving you with effectively 9 turns to do it
  • BG-86: When a game ends, players with potion effects end with negative of that stat

Front-end modifications

  • Moved spells, weapons, and location to playerData
  • Changed lockout progress and lockout challenges to hashmaps

Version 2.7.7 Release

Posted 9:42pm on February 9, 2024


A 2-in-1 update! 2 major features added

Note that this update is waiting for Tourney Group Game 1 to terminate to be released

Additions

  • Potions: Purchase them for temporary buffs!
  • AOE Spells: Cast a spell onto everyone on a single island!

Potions

  • During your turn, you may purchase and consume potions whenever you want!
  • Potions give you temporary buffs for some amount of buffs. However, most potions have a level requirement for them to be used
  • You can only have one potion of each type active at once. For example, you cannot drink another healing potion if you already drank one in the previous turn
  • This is the only way to go above the 80 AP limit on some stats!

AOE Spells

  • You can launch these spells and hit every player on the island you choose!
  • You deal the same amount of damage to every player on that island
  • Just like other spells, AOE spells cost mana to use and can only be used once per turn

Changes

  • The "extra damage" perk from Strength has been nerfed from +1% per Strength -> +0.5% per strength
    • Note: This does NOT affect the "extra crit damage" perk from Strength - that stays the same (+1% Crit Damage per Strength)
  • Weather has been balanced

Version 2.7 Release

Posted 8:13pm on January 19, 2024


Version 2.7 - To the Skies! Has been released!!!

Additions

  • Islands: Players will start and end their turns on an island. There are a maximum of 25 islands possible (potentially more later)
  • Bridges: This is the only way to traverse between islands! They take up a fraction of a melee attack, which makes melee attacks deal less damage

Bridges

  • When you build a bridge, there is a 10% chance the bridge ends up being a t(r)oll
  • Toll bridges cost ability points in the following formula: The minimum distance to travel between the start and end islands on free bridges, divided by 2, rounded up

  • You can cross a maximum of 5 bridges before you "run out of breath"
  • Bridges are bidirectional!

Changes

  • You can only attack a player if you are on the same island as them
  • You can now melee attack multiple players on the same turn!
  • The amount of xp required to level up has been reverted
  • Upon entering the gulag your mana regen is now set to 1
  • Upon entering the gulag your max health is now set to 10

Fixes
